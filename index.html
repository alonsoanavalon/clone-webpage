Ideation

Finding creativity within

Once you know who you are and understand how to stay consistent over time, it is important to remain fresh and relevant in the eyes of the consumer. Based on your choices made via the community brand method - whether you figured them out by yourself via our free online test or through our coaching program – we will give you the handles to stay creative within the boundaries of your own unique identity.

What

A step-by-step approach to keep your unique brand identity relevant and fresh over time.

Why

Powerful and compelling creativity builds on your brand’s identity.

Who

All managers who are involved in the executional management of the brand.

Why ideation?

Because all it takes is a fresh perspective

By looking at your brand through fresh eyes, you will uncover new opportunities. During our ideation sessions we hand you different tools that trigger your creativity, without compromising your long-term brand identity.
At the end of our workshop you will have an overview of strong and creative storylines that are the basis for your communication across channels.
All these creative ideas will contribute to the visibility of your brand and will make your brand stronger in the long run.
